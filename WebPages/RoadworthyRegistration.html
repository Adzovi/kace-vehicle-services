RoadWorthy Registration

Welcome. Scroll down to start your Roadworthy Registration process

Steps to obtain roadworthy certificate

  • Take your vehicle for inspection at a private garage.
  • Provide original vehicle and inspection documents. This is for a new vehicle
  • For an already registered vehicle, you will need only the old roadworthy certificate.
  • Fill the form and provide any necessary information.
  • Make Payments
  • Book an appointment for collection or request for delivery. (Includes extra charges).
  • The documents will be reviewed and processed. you will receive a notification if any errors are detected.
