Vehicle Registration

Click to Begin
Steps To Register Your Vehicle:
  • Take your vehicle to CEPS for certification of vehicle documents.
  • Take your vehicle to a private garage for inspection. eg. Kim
  • Upload CEPS documents and other required documents.
  • Fill all forms and provide any required information.
  • An invoice will be generated with details of your transaction
  • Finalize Registration.
  • Load Receipt and Proof of registration and present for collection of registration plate.
  • Payment will be made at our accounts office
  • Any errors made during registration will be rectified

RoadWorthy Registration

Click to Begin
Steps To Obtain Roadworthy Documents
  • Take your vehicle for inspection at a private garage.
  • Provide original vehicle and inspection documents. This is for a new vehicle
  • For an already registered vehicle, you will need only the old roadworthy certificate.
  • Fill the form and provide any necessary information.
  • Make Payments
  • Book an appointment for collection .
  • The documents will be reviewed and processed. you will receive a notification is any errors are detected.
